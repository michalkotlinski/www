The Ethereum Computer
Your Passport to the Internet of Value

Turning personal assets into income

The Ethereum Computer is a tiny, preconfigured server running an optimized blockchain node and a series of decentralized applications. It brings smart contract technology to the entire home by making it possible to rent access to any space or smart object and accept payments without intermediaries.

Flexible, the Ethereum computer is ideal for both consumers and developers, providing a platform to securely browse any web 3.0 app from the convenience of your desktop or home theater, alongside an always up-to-date stack to develop Ethereum applications.

In the future, it will become a source of passive income: it's the ideal platform for Ethereum Proof of Stake mining, and will come preloaded with experimental apps, for example to rent your hard drive space in exchange of cryptocurrencies.

The first developer kits will be available Q4 2016.

Developer Friendly

Develop applications with Ethereum and never have to worry about installation difficulties. We’ll sort out updates and compatibility issues between the various components of the Ethereum 'stack' (Whisper, EVM, Web3.js, Swarm, etc), and will push updates only when we have tested the framework top to bottom.

The Slock.it stack is completely open-source: our modular, event based framework can be extended to control any smart contracts driven embedded devices on the Ethereum blockchain.
