Roadmap

June 2015

The Story Begins

From the day of the project's inception, our team will spend the following months in stealth mode relentlessly developing, building and testing prototypes.

November 12th 2015

Slock.it Revealed

The project is introduced at Devcon One in front of 400 blockchain developers and enthusiasts. Our first product, the SHS (now renamed the 'Ethereum Computer'), is announced, and demoed opening doors and powering up electrical appliances.

Early 2016

Slock token presale

An innovative presale to bootstrap the world's first DAO. Operating entirely autonomously and transparently, the DAO will elect a service provider to execute on a series of core objectives in the physical world, to be reviewed at regular intervals.

2017

Launch!

The first Slocks enter the market. To reward the participants who helped bring it to life, the DAO issued Slock Tokens granting rewards whenever Slocks are put to use.

A Bright Future

A World of a 100 Billion Devices

We started Slock.it with realistic targets and the simplest of all use cases: the humble lock, or ‘slock’, consuming very little of the processing power available to today's SoCs.

Slock.it therefore lends itself very well to deeper IoT integration, both in the commercial and industrial sectors, where we are piloting networks of autonomous devices operating as part of an Economy of Things, engaging in contracts with each other, paying each other for their services, yet never having to be centrally managed.

Slock.it lleva los beneficios de la cadena de bloques -transparencia, seguridad y auditabilidad- al mundo de los objetos reales.

Nuestra tecnología puede adaptarse a casi cualquier aparato y los primeros prototipos ya se encuentran en manos de desarrolladores.
